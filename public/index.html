About Threadline

Threadline is a streamlined platform designed to support your efforts in growing your group’s catalog and designing clothing from the ground up. Our primary goal is to help Roblox clothing group owners reach new markets, simplify development and testing workflows, and gain easy access to any templates they’d like to utilize—all in one centralized location.


One of our core features is the Roblox Classic Clothing Template Downloader, which allows you to easily download any Roblox Classic Clothing template directly from our homepage. If you’d rather not visit the website every time, we also offer the Threadline Web Extension, available on both the Mozilla Add-ons Store and the Chrome Web Store. With the extension, you can access the Template Downloader directly from your browser, along with additional tools such as the Group Template Downloader.


Threadline also offers an exclusive experience through Threadline Premium. Premium users enjoy a completely ad-free environment, custom whitespace converters, and access to sponsored listings visible to all Threadline users. With thousands of impressions available each day for just $3.99 a month, you can promote your group, catalog item, or game without the headache of managing cost-per-click calculations, budgets, or wasted ad spend. Instead, you gain direct visibility among a focused community of creators.


In short, Threadline is built to save you time, reduce friction, and give you the tools you need to create, grow, and showcase your work—so you can focus on designing, not troubleshooting.
