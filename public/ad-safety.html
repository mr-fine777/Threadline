To comply with our advertising providers’ Terms of Service, we have lifted the ad shield to allow users to click on our ads. Here at Threadline, we understand the risks in doing so, and would like to go over some ad safety rules for our users.
Your safety and privacy matter to us. While we carefully select reputable advertising partners, Threadline does not directly control the content of third-party advertisements. Please review the following guidelines before interacting with any ad:
• Do not share personal information (passwords, banking details, Social Security numbers, verification codes, etc.) with any advertiser.
• Be cautious of urgency tactics, such as “limited time only,” “act now,” or threats involving account suspension.
• Avoid downloading software or files from ads unless you have independently verified the source.
• Never send money, cryptocurrency, gift cards, or wire transfers to an advertiser.
• Double-check URLs before entering any information. Look for secure domains (https://) and ensure the site name matches the official company.
• If an ad redirects unexpectedly, asks for unusual permissions, or appears misleading, close the page immediately.
If you encounter a suspicious advertisement, please report it to our support team so we can review and take appropriate action.
By clicking on ads, you acknowledge that you are interacting with third-party content outside of Threadline’s direct control. We encourage all users to practice safe browsing habits and to use updated antivirus and browser protection tools.
Thank you for helping us keep Threadline a safe and secure platform for everyone.
